Benefit to Colleges
  • Admissionx.com is an online platform for elite colleges to showcase themselves
  • Colleges can fill up their seat through our website because all over country students will enroll through our website to check/enquire their dream colleges according to city of their choice by just a click away.
  • Colleges can also revise their fee structure and seat availability etc., on day to day basis or first come first basis similar to airline companies or make my trip booking sites from lower to higher slab.
  • Colleges get two choices Colleges can either get their admissions done through our website or can showcase their Institute for enquiries from students showing interest in their college through our website.

College Fee Structure: It is mandatory to maintain the same fee structure as mentioned on our site for all the years, after enrollment of student at their institutes. Colleges are advised to fill up their seat on 1st come 1st basis from lower to higher slab.

Our charges:We charge students a fee of Rs. 499, which forms a part of the college fees. Rest of the fee will be deposited by the student to your institute directly along with the registration letter.

Document Required Online for Colleges: AICTE/UNIVERSITY/PCI approval letter along with legal documents.

Branding/Publicity: The website can be viewed through different media type’s i.e. T.V/News paper/On air etc.
